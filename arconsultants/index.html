Why Records and Information Management Matters?

Malawi's success, both in the public and private sectors, is dependent on effective record-keeping and information management. As a result, our country's socio-economic and technical improvements necessitate expertise in record and information management.

Furthermore, the Access to Information Act requires public and commercial institutions to maintain flawless records management standards in order to better serve the people. However, data suggests worrying neglect in record maintenance in Malawi's governmental and private institutions, owing mostly to a lack of experience in records and information administration.
